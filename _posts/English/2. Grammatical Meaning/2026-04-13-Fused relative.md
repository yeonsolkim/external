## What-NP
fused relative에서 what은 pronoun이며, 상위 clause의 constituent(antecedent)로서의 function과 relative clause의 constituent(relativized element)로서의 function을 동시에 수행한다. 

**what proved to be the oldest fossil whale**
* NP
  * head(pronoun): what
  * modifier(relative clause): proved to be the oldest fossil whale
    * head(V): proved (be-passive)
    * complement(to-infinitival clause): to be the oldest fossil whale
