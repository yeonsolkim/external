## 1. Auxiliary verb의 complement로 기능하는 past-participial clause
#### I was invited.
이 경우 be는 modal auxiliaries이므로 transparent verb이고, 따라서 raising이 일어난 구조로 볼 수 있다.  
* raised subject: i
* predicate
  * head(auxiliary verb): was
  * complement(past-participial clause): invited
    * predicand: i
    * predicate: invited


> Simplification: (I) was / ~ invited.


## 2. Modifier로 기능하는 past-participial clause
#### rocks formed from river deposits
* np
  * head: rocks
  * modifier(past-participial clause): formed from river deposits
     * predicand: rocks
     * predicate: formed from river deposits
