supplement는 non-restrictive이다. <br>

### 1. NP의 Supplement로 기능하는 Past-Participial Clause
**The Strange Situation Experiment, conducted by psychologist Mary Ainsworth in 1970s, determined the attachment behavior of infants toward their caregivers.**
* Subject(NP)
  * Head: The Strange Situation Experiment
  * Supplement(Past-Participial Clause): conducted by psychologist Mary Ainsworth in 1970s
    * Head: conducted
    * Complement(PP): by psychologist Mary Ainsworth
    * Modifier(PP): in 1970s
* Predicate
  * Head: determined
  * Complement: the attachment behavior of infants toward their caregivers
    * Determiner: the
    * Modifier(NP): attachment
    * Head: behavior
    * Complement(PP): of infants
    * Modifier(PP): toward their caregivers


> Simplification: The Strange Situation Experiment, conducted / by psychologist Mary Ainsworth / in 1970s, determined / the attachment behavior / of infants / toward their caregivers.


### 2. NP의 Supplement로 기능하는 Finite Clause Functioning 
**The correct approach, it was realized only much later, is to consider the finite situation**
* Subject(NP)
  * Determiner(Determinative): The
  * Modifier(Adj): correct
  * Head: approach
  * Supplement(Finite Clause): it was realized only much later
