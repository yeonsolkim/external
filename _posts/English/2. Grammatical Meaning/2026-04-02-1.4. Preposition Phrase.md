## Property
1. PP는 complement를 가지지 않을 수 있다. e.g., aboard, outside, when, where
2. PP는 VP, NP, AdjP의 dependent가 될 수 있다. 

--- 

## 1. In NP
1. Complement in NP
2. Subject Corresponding Complement
3. Object Corresponding Complement 


> Examples.
> 1. three of the boys
> 2. the rise of the sun
> 3. the conquest of the world


## 2. In VP
> Examples.<br>
> She saw it in the rearview mirror.
