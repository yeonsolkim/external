## Properties
1. Prepositions은 전형적으로 NP를 complement로 license하지만 PP, AdvP, clause 등 other complement types도 가능하다. 
2. PPs는 complements를 가지지 않을 수 있다. e.g., aboard, outside, when, where
3. PPs는 VP, NP, AdjP에서 dependents가 될 수 있다. 

--- 

## 1. Dependents of nouns
1. complements
2. subject-corresponding complements
3. object-corresponding complements 


> Examples.
> 1. three of the boys
> 2. the rise of the sun
> 3. the conquest of the world


## 2. Dependents of verbs
> Examples.<br>
> She saw it in the rearview mirror.

## 3. Dependents of adjectives
>Examples.<br>
>I fell so ill with hay fever.
