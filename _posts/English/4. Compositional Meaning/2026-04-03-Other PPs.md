## 2. The dependent of a verb
1. circumstance
2. manner(동작 과정의 양태)
3. consequential purpose
4. content
5. means
6. reason
7. unit
> Examples.
> 1. This emerged [during the war]: 상황적 배경(또는 시간적 배경).
> 2. He solved the problem [with great difficulty]: 그는 많은 우여곡절 끝에 문제를 풀었다.
> 3. He uses his power [to bad effect].
> 4. The conference was acclaimed [as a considerable success].
   >5. They ascertained exactly [as a result of research or calculation].
   >6. it was marked [by a good or legitimate reason].<br>
   >   They judge people [by their clothes].
   >7. They are paid [by the week].
