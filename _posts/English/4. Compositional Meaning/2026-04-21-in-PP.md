PP 내에서 In은 거의 대부분 NP를 complement로 license한다.

### 1. Functioning in an NP
1. spatial container 
2. temporal container
3. the state something is in
4. the group someone belongs to

> Examples.
> 1. some water in the glass
> 2. exercising in the morning
> 3. a girl in love
> 4. a member in the committee


### 2. Functioning in a VP
1. spatial background
2. temporal background
3. manner
4. cause
5. domain

>Examples.
>1. He studied in the room.
>2. Einstein provided empirical evidence for the atomic theory in 1905.
>3. He answered in English.
>4. She stepped back in surprise.



### 3. Functioning as the complement of an AdjP
1. domain


>Examples.
>1. The country is rich in natural resources.<br>
>   He was weak in math.
