### 1. Modifier in NP
1. Attribute 
>a man of courage


2. Carrier 
>the color of the sky


3. Origin
>a man of noble birth


4. The whole to which that part belongs 
>the handle of this spot (physical whole)<br>
>skeletons of whales (anatomical whole)<br>
>the innermost depths of the human soul (spatial whole; metaphor)


5. Content, Domain
>the problem of the low birth rate<br>
>the way of life <br>
>the world of individual emotions<br>
>by an effort of memory (memory에 관한 effort)


6. Possessor 
>the assets of a nation


7. Cause
>the shock of the news

8. Composition (조성, 구성 성분)
>a wall of stone<br>
>a morass of complicated mathematical equations


9. Producer
>the works of Shakespeare


10. Apposition
>the city of Rome


11. Relative
> the brother of John

### 2. Modifier in VP
1. Origin
   >He was born of a wealthy family.
   
2. Ingredient
>That house is made of brick.
