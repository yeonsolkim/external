### Syllogism
#### 1. Modus ponens
1. $p\Rightarrow q.$
2. $p$ is true.
3. Therefore $q$ is true.

#### 2. Modus tollens
1. $p\Rightarrow q.$
2. $\neg q$ is true.
3. Therefore $\neg p$ is true.
