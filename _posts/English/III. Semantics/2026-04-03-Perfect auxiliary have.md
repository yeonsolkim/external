Perfect auxiliary have는 complement로 past-participial clause를 license한다.

## 1. Plain form
$(UT \subset TT) \land (ET < TT)$: topic time은 현재, **event time은 과거**.<br>

core message: 
1. 과거 event의 importance of the present. e.g., He has broken his leg.
2. 현재의 경험. e.g., I have been to Japan.
3. 과거 event인데 현재도 동일. e.g., She has lived here for ten years.
4. 과거 event가 현재 바로 직전에 발생. e.g., I have just finished my homework.
5. 과거에 수차례 반복. e.g., We have visited him several times.

## 2. Preterite
$ET < TT < UT$: topic time은 과거, **event time은 대과거**.<br>
