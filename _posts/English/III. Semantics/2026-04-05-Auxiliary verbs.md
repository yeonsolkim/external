### 1. Can
1. ability
2. permission
3. **circumstantial possibility**
4. **conjecture**

>Examples.
>1. He can lift 500N.
>2. You can go home now.
>3. Even expert drivers can make mistakes.<br>
>   Water can boil.<br>
>   The absence of legs cannot disguise their affinities with mammals.<br>
>   I can't believe that we are faced with that sort of choice. (화자의 epistemic state에 의해 제한된 possibility) <br>
>4. She can't be tired now. 

### 2. May
1. permission
2. conjecture
3. **circumstantial possibility**

### 3. Must, have 
1. duty: 어떤 주체가 해야할 의무
2. **necessity**
3. strong conjecture

>Examples. 
>1. You must do military service.
>2. I have to call my mom.
>3. He must be hungry.

### 4. Will
1. **will** (의지)
2. **cooperativeness**
3. **disposition** (경향)
4. 습성 (습관적)
5. definite conjecture

>Examples. 
>1. I will lose weight.
>2. The door won't open.
>3. After sad things, happy things will happen. (슬픈 일 뒤에는 행복한일이 생기기 마련이다.)
>4. I would sometimes travel alone. (나는 때로 혼자 여행을 하곤 했다.)
>5. He will be a doctor.


### 5. Should, ought
1. **expectation**: normality와 상식을 기반으로 한 일반적 expectation
2. duty


> Examples.
> 1. It should be obvious that humans walk on two feet.
