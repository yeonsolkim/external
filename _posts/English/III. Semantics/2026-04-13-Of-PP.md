### 1. Functioning in an NP
1. attribute 
2. carrier 
3. origin
4. the whole it belongs to
5. content, domain
6. possessor 
7. cause
8. composition (조성, 구성 성분)
9. the time it belongs to
10. producer
11. apposition
12. relative


>Examples.
>1. a man of courage
>2. the color of the sky
>3. a man of noble birth
>4. the handle of this spot (physical whole)<br>
>   skeletons of whales (anatomical whole)<br>
>   the innermost depths of the human soul (spatial whole; metaphor)
>5. the problem of the low birth rate<br>
>   the way of life <br>
>   the world of individual emotions<br>
>   by an effort of memory (memory에 관한 effort)
>6. the assets of a nation

>8. a wall of stone<br>

>9. the winter of 1924–1925
>10. the works of Shakespeare
>11. the city of Rome
