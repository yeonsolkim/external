## Control, inhibition, inevitability
1. **cannot help $+$ gerund-participial clause**,<br>**cannot but $+$ bare-infinitival clause:** <br>low control, failed inhibition, inevitability
2. **be bound $+$ to-infinitival clause:** <br>situational inevitability
3. **burst out $+$ gerund-participial clause:** <br>failed inhibition
