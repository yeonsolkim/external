## 2. The dependent of a verb
1. background
2. manner(동작 과정의 양태)
3. consequential purpose
4. content
5. means
6. reason
7. unit
8. determining factor


> Examples.
> 1. This emerged <u>during the war</u>: circumstantial (temporal interpretation도 가능) <br>
>    We join Wilson in 1911 <u>as he wonders whether he can re-create the scene in the laboratory</u>: circumstantial
> 2. He solved the problem <u>with great difficulty</u>: 그는 많은 우여곡절 끝에 문제를 풀었다.
> 3. He uses his power <u>to bad effect</u>.
> 4. The conference was acclaimed <u>as a considerable success</u>.
   >5. They ascertained exactly <u>as a result of research or calculation</u>.
   >6. It was marked <u>by a good or legitimate reason</u>.<br>
   >   It worked out <u>with her help</u>.
   >7. They are paid <u>by the week</u>.
   >8. My scheme would stand or fall <u>by that principle</u>.
