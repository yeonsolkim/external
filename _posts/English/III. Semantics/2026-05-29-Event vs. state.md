### 1. Eventive interpretation
**I was invited.**
* Raised Subject: I
* Predicate
  * Head(Auxiliary Verb): was
  * Complement(Past-Participial Clause): invited


### 2. Stative interpretation 
**Her phone was broken.**
* Subject: Her phone
* Predicate
  * Head(Lexical Verb): was
  * Complement(AdjP): broken
