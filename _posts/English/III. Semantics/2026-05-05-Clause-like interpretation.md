### 1. NPs
verb의 argument structure가 genitive 형태로 실현된다.

#### 1.1. event nouns
e.g., walk, trip, conversation, fight, arrival, meeting, decision, attempt<br>

examples.
1. the enemy's destruction of the city<br>
   → The enemy destroyed the city.
2. I always remember a mountain walk with some friends<br>
   → I walked in the mountains with some friends.
3. my attempt to apply this scheme to the hydrogen atom<br>
   → I attempted to apply this scheme to the hydrogen atom.

  
#### 1.2. state nouns
e.g., relationship, marriage, alliance, rivalry; anger, pleasure<br>
examples.
1. John’s friendship with Mary surprised everyone<br>
   → John was friends with Mary.
2. her anger at the decision<br>
   → She was angry at the decision.


### 2. Object-oriented predicative complements
examples.
1. I found myself in an impenetrable morass<br>
   → myself was in an impenetrable morass.
2. to put oneself back in the position<br> 
   → oneself is in the position.
