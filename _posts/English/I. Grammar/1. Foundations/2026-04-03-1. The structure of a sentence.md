### 1. Overview
* a sentence의 a part를 constituent라고 하자.
* a constituent는 더 큰 constituent 안에서 a particular function을 가진다.

### 2. Constituents
1. lexeme
	* verb 
	   * lexical verb
	   * auxiliary verb
	* noun 
	* determinative 
	* adjective 
	* adverb 
	* preposition
	* subordinator 
	* coordinator
2. phrase (whose head is a lexeme)
   * verb phrase
   * noun phrase
   * determinative phrase
   * adjective phrase
   * adverbial phrase
   * preposition phrase
1. clause 
2. coordination 

### 3. Functions
definition: 더 큰 constituent 안에서의 기능
1. in a coordination: coordinate, marker
2. in a clause: predicate(VP), dependent
3. in a phrase: head, dependent


### 4. Dependents of a clause
1. subject
2. adjunct: predicate가 요구하지 않는 optional dependent.
3. marker


### 5. Dependents of a phrase
1. complement
    * object: subject와 다른 한 대상이면 object.
       * direct object
       * indirect object
     * predicative complement
2. adjunct: head가 요구하지 않는 optional dependent.
    * modifier: fully integrated, attached
    * supplement: loosely attached
3. determiner
