### 1. Classification of clauses by finiteness
1. finite clause
   * 대체로 subject, tense, agreement를 갖는다.   
1. nonfinite clause
   * tense나 agreement 정보를 보이지 않는다. (단, aspect인 perfect는 가질 수 있다.)
   * subjectless인 경우가 많다. (hollow인 경우도 있다.)
   * 항상 subordinate이다. 

#### Subclasses of nonfinite clauses
1. to-infinitival clause
2. bare infinitival clause
3. gerund-participial clause
4. past-participial clause
5. verbless clause


---

### 2. Classification of clauses by characteristic speech act
1. declarative clause: making a statement
2. closed interrogative clause
3. open interrogative clause
4. exclamative clause: making an exclamatory statement
5. imperative clause: issuing a directive

---

### 3. Classes of clause occurrences
1. matrix: the one immediately containing the subordinate; relatively larger
2. subordinate: the relatively smaller one

#### Subclasses of subordinate
1. relative 
2. comparative 
3. content
