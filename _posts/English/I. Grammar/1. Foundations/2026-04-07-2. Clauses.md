## 2. Classification of clauses by characteristic speech act
1. declarative clause: making a statement
2. closed interrogative clause
3. open interrogative clause
4. exclamative clause: making an exclamatory statement
5. imperative clause: issuing a directive

## 3. Classes of clause occurrences
1. matrix: the one immediately containing the subordinate; relatively larger
2. subordinate: the relatively smaller one

1. relative 
2. comparative
