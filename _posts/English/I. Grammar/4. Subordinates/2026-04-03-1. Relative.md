### Properties
1. finite clause이다. (다만, semantic gap은 존재)
2. subordinate clause이다. 
3. function은 noun의 modifier 또는 supplement이다. 

### Categories
1. wh relative clause
2. that relative clause
3. bare relative clause: gap이 object이다. supplement로는 기능할 수 없다.

>Examples. 
>1. My brother, whom I met yesterday, is a doctor.
>2. the book (that I bought)
>3. The book (I bought) / was (informative).<br>
>   I / sold (what (I made)).


### Wh relative clause
wh relative word: head noun과 anaphoric relation을 맺고, relative clause안에서 하나의 constituent로서 기능한다.
1. who, which (pronoun): preposition fronting이 같이 일어나면 formal한 의미를 준다.
2. when, where (preposition): relative clause안에서 adjunct of time 또는 adjunct of place으로 기능. 
3. why (adverb)
4. whose (determinative): determiner의 head도 함께 앞으로 나온다. 

>Examples.
>1.  a quantity chosen as (a) standard (in terms of which other quantities may be expressed)<br>
>   → other quantities may be expressed in terms of <u>which</u><br>
>2. (the) day (when he was born)<br>
>   → he was born <u>when</u>
>3. (the) reason (why they came)<br>
>   → they came <u>why</u>
>4. (the) people (whose house she wants to rent)<br>
>   → she wants to rent <u>whose</u> house<br>
>   in a way whose aptness he would instantly have recognized
