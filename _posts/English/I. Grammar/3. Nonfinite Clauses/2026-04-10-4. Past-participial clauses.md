## 1. Functioning as the complement of a auxiliary verb
**I was invited.** 
* raised subject: I
* predicate
  * head(auxiliary verb): was
  * complement(past-participial clause): invited

## 2. Functioning as an adjunct 
past-participial clauses가 adjunct로 기능할 때 passive meaning을 가질 수 있다.<br>
**rocks formed from river deposits**
* NP
  * head: rocks
  * modifier(past-participial clause): formed from river deposits
     * predicand: rocks
     * predicate: formed from river deposits

**Seen from my perspective, there is much to learn.**
* supplement(past-participial clause)
  * predicand: the situation
  * predicate: seen from my perspective
