## 1. Past-participial clauses functioning as the complement of a auxiliary verb
**I was invited.** 
* raised subject: I
* predicate
  * head(auxiliary verb): was
  * complement(past-participial clause): invited


> Semantics: (I) was invited.


## 2. Past-participial clauses functioning as an adjunct 
past-participial clauses가 adjunct로 기능할 때 passive meaning을 가질 수 있다. 즉, 때때로 finite relative clause의 passive counterpart와 의미적으로 대응한다.<br>
**rocks formed from river deposits**
* np
  * head: rocks
  * modifier(past-participial clause): formed from river deposits
     * predicand: rocks
     * predicate: formed from river deposits
