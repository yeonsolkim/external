Do 등을 통하여, 앞선 VP를 가리킬 수 있다. 이를 anaphora라고 하는데, 사전적 정의는 다음과 같다:<br>
the use of a word referring to or replacing a word used earlier in a sentence, to avoid repetition, such as do.

**I don’t know if she has seen it yet, but she may have done.**
* but she may have done
  * may → modality (conjecture)
  * have → perfect aspect
  * done → 'see it' 

**I made much swifter progress than I would have done in Göttingen.**
* complement(comparative clause): than I would have done in Göttingen
  * would → modal remoteness
  * have → perfect
  * done → 'make progress'

**She did better in the exam than we’d thought she would do**
* complement(comparative clause): than we had thought she would do
  * marker(subordinator): than
  * subject: we
  * predicate: had thought she would do
    * had → perfect + past tense
    * complement: she would do
      * would → modal remoteness
      * do → 'do in the exam'
