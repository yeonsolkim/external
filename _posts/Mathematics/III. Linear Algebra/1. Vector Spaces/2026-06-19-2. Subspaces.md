**Definition 1.2.1.** A subset $\mathbb W$ of a vector space $\mathbb V$ over a field $\mathbb F$ is called a *subspace* of $\mathbb V$ if $\mathbb W$ is a vector space over $\mathbb F$ with the operations of addition and scalar multiplication defined on $\mathbb V.$
