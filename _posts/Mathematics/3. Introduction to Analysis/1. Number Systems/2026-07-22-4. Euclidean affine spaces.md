**Definition 1.4.1.** Let $n\in\mathbb N$ be positive. If $x=(x_1,\dots,x_n)$ and $y=(y_1,\dots,y_n)$ are elements of $\mathbb R^n,$ and $\lambda\in\mathbb R,$ we define the *coordinatewise operations*
$$
x+y=(x_1+y_1,\dots,x_n+y_n),  
\qquad
x-y=(x_1-y_1,\dots,x_n-y_n),  
$$

and
$$
\lambda x = 
(\lambda x_1,\dots,\lambda x_n).  
$$

We also define the *zero tuple* by

$$  
0_{\mathbb R^n}=(0,\dots,0).  
$$
