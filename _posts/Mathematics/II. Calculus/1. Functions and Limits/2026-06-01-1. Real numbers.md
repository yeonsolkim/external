**Definition 1.1** (*Absolute Value*).

$$
\vert x\vert  :=
\begin{cases}
x, & x \ge 0,\\
-x, & x < 0.
\end{cases}
$$

<br> 

**Theorem 1.2.**

1. $\vert x\vert  \ge 0$
2. $\vert xy\vert  = \vert x\vert \cdot \vert y\vert$
3. $\vert x+y\vert  \le \vert x\vert +\vert y\vert$
4. $\vert x\vert ^2 = x^2$
5. $\vert x\vert  = 0 \Longrightarrow x = 0$

<br>

**Definition 1.3** (*Square Root*).

$$
\sqrt{x}=y :\Longleftrightarrow y \ge 0,\ y^2=x
$$

<br>

**Theorem 1.4.**

1. $\sqrt{x^2}=\vert x\vert$
2. $(\sqrt{x})^2=x$

<br>

**Definition 1.5.**

1. Harmonic mean: for $x+y\ne 0$,

	$$
	HM(x,y):=\frac{2xy}{x+y}
	$$

2. Geometric mean: for $xy\ge 0$,

	$$
	GM(x,y):=\sqrt{xy}
	$$

3. Arithmetic mean: 

	$$
	AM(x,y):=\frac{x+y}{2}
	$$

4. Quadratic mean:

	$$
	QM(x,y):=\sqrt{\frac{x^2+y^2}{2}}
	$$

5. Weighted arithmetic mean: for $w_i\ge 0$ and $\sum_{i=1}^{n}w_i=1$,

	$$
	WAM(x_1,\ldots,x_n):=\sum_{i=1}^{n}w_i x_i
	$$

<br>

**Theorem 1.6.**

$$
\min(x_1,\ldots,x_n) \le \operatorname{mean}(x_1,\ldots,x_n) \le \max(x_1,\ldots,x_n)
$$

<br>

**Theorem 1.7.**

$$
HM \le GM \le AM \le QM
$$
