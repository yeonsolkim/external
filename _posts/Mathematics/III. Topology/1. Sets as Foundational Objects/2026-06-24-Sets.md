**Remark 1.1.1.** We take sets as foundational objects and we shall redefine some mathematical objects based on sets. This is because, although we will be working on object-level, we should observe ZFC to avoid fatal logical errors, and also because the objects redefined in such way are equivalent to the ordinary objects defined before.
