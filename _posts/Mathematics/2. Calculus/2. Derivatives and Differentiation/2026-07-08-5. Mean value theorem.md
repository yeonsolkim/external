**Theorem 2.5.1** (Rolle's theorem). Let $f$ be a function that is continuous on $[a,b]$ and differentiable on $(a,b).$ If $f(a)=f(b)$ then, 

$$ \exists c\in (a,b)\ (f'(c)=0).$$

*Proof.* 
<br>

**Theorem 2.5.2** (Mean value theorem).
