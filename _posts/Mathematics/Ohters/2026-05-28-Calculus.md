**Definition of limit.** 

$$\lim_{x\to a}f(x)=L\Longleftrightarrow \forall \varepsilon >0\ \exists \delta >0\ \forall x\,\left( 0<|x-a|<\ \delta \rightarrow |f(x)-L|<\varepsilon\right)$$
