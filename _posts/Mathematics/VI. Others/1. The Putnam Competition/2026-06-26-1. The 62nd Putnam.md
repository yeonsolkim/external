**A1.** Consider a set $S$ and a binary operation $\ast,$ that is, for each $a,b\in S, a\ast b\in S.$ Assume $(a\ast b)\ast a = b$ for all $a,b\in S.$ Prove that $a\ast (b\ast a)=b$ for all $a,b\in S.$<br><br>
*Proof.* We have $((b\ast a)\ast b)\ast (b\ast a)=b.$ But we also have $(b\ast a)\ast b=a.$ Thus $a\ast  (b\ast a)=b.$<span class="qed">$\square$</span><br>
